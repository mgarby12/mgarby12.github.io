Video Making Process

I collaborated an agent to produce video walkthroughs for active property listings. Using specialized stabilization equipment, I captured multi-angle footage of each property to ensure a smooth, professional viewer experience. Post-production, I analyzed my best takes and edited within Wondershare Filmora, focusing on pacing and visual flow. I uploaded the final products across professional Instagram and Facebook channels with engaging, brand-aligned captions to drive property engagement and social media reach.
